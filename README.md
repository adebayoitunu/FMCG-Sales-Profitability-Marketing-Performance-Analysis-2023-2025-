## Analysis Overview

This project analyzes sales performance, profitability, and marketing efficiency using an FMCG dataset spanning 2023–2025. The objective was to identify revenue drivers, evaluate product profitability, assess marketing effectiveness, and provide actionable recommendations to improve business performance. Interactive dashboards were developed in Power BI to support strategic decision-making.

## Executive Summary

This project analyzes the sales, profitability, and marketing performance of a Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) business between 2023 and 2025 using Power BI. The objective was to identify revenue drivers, profit opportunities, marketing effectiveness, and operational inefficiencies to support data-driven business decisions.

The analysis revealed that while overall revenue remained strong, profitability varied significantly across product categories, regions, and promotional strategies. By evaluating key performance indicators and business trends, the project identified opportunities to optimize marketing investment, prioritize high-margin products, improve regional performance, and strengthen long-term profitability.

The recommendations presented are designed to help management improve resource allocation, maximize return on investment, and support sustainable business growth.

## Objective

The analysis aimed to answer the following questions:

## Sales Performance

- How have sales evolved over time?
- Which channels, regions, and sales representatives drive revenue?
- Which markets offer growth opportunities?

## Profitability Analysis

- Which products and categories contribute most to profit?
- How do COGS, logistics costs, and marketing spend affect profitability?
- Which brands deliver the highest profit margins?

## Marketing & Efficiency

- Which promotion types provide the highest return on investment?
- Which products generate the highest and lowest marketing ROI?
- Is marketing spending aligned with performance?

#### Strengthen Digital Channels

#### Prepare for Seasonal Demand

Inventory planning and marketing activities should be aligned with year-end demand spikes to maximize revenue opportunities.

#### Protect High-Performing Markets

Maintain strong customer relationships and market presence in Europe and North America to preserve the company's primary revenue sources.

### Recommendations

#### Prioritize High-Profit Products

Increase inventory availability and marketing support for products such as Laundry Detergent and Shampoo Repair, as they contribute disproportionately to total profit.

#### Optimize Low-Performing Products

Conduct periodic reviews of products with low profit contributions to determine whether pricing adjustments, cost reductions, or portfolio rationalization are required.

#### Strengthen Household and Personal Care Categories

Given their strong profitability, these categories should receive additional investment and expansion efforts.

#### Review Beverage Cost Efficiency

Investigate why Beverage products incur the highest costs and identify opportunities to improve supply chain efficiency and pricing strategies.

#### Evaluate Brand Performance

Analyze the underlying factors driving low margins among underperforming brands and develop strategies to improve profitability.

#### Focus on Profit, Not Revenue Alone

Incorporate profitability measures alongside revenue metrics when assessing product performance to ensure decisions are based on value creation rather than sales volume.

### Recommendations

#### Reallocate Marketing Budgets

Shift resources toward products and promotion types that consistently generate stronger returns.

#### Review Low-Performing Campaigns

Evaluate Festival Campaigns and other low-ROI initiatives to identify opportunities for improvement or budget reduction.

#### Support High-ROI Products

Increase marketing support for products demonstrating strong returns to maximize efficiency.

#### Focus on Marketing Effectiveness

Future marketing decisions should prioritize ROI rather than marketing spend alone.

#### Investigate Low-ROI Products

Products generating weak returns should be reviewed for pricing, positioning, and campaign effectiveness before additional investments are made.

## Expected Business Impact

If the recommendations from this analysis are implemented, the business can expect to:

- Improve marketing return on investment by reallocating resources toward high-performing promotional channels.
- Increase profitability by prioritizing high-margin product categories and reviewing low-performing products.
- Strengthen regional performance through targeted expansion and localized marketing strategies.
- Optimize inventory planning by aligning stock levels with product demand and profitability trends.
- Support executive decision-making through continuous KPI monitoring and data-driven performance evaluation.

## Limitations

This analysis was conducted using historical sales data from 2023 to 2025. While it provides valuable insights into business performance, several limitations should be considered:

- Customer demographic information was unavailable, limiting customer segmentation analysis.
- External factors such as inflation, competitor activity, and seasonal market conditions were not included.
- The analysis focused on descriptive business intelligence rather than predictive forecasting.
- Results are dependent on the quality and completeness of the provided dataset.

Future work could incorporate forecasting models, customer segmentation, and predictive analytics to support more proactive business decision-making.

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how business intelligence and interactive dashboards can turn raw FMCG data into meaningful insights that support informed decision-making and continuous business improvement.
